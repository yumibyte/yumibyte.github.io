For the flyer project for EE285: Embedded Systems Workshop, I was the Boards Team Lead where it was our goal to make bringing-up the PCBs required to drive the motors/lights of the "flyers" (which look like airplanes) consistent.

The overall goal of these flyers was to make an art installation in the stairwell of Packard Electrical Engineering Building. Each flyer has a PCB, for which I helped make a test plan for, bring-up the necessary boards for the quarter, and adjust the PCB design based on flaws we found from testing.
